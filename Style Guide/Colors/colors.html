Neu UI's Color Scheme

Neu UI comes with pre-defined colors, which are consistent in the overall design. The colors can be accessed by pre-defined classes. You can also reset the colors by defining them in your root.

Default Palette

Below is the color palette, used by Neu UI, listed with the hex-codes.

Primary #001f3f
Secondary #00468c
Background #a5cdd5
Warning #363636
Error #4c0c09
Success #0a2e0a
